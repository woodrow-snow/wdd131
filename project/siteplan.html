Woodrow Snow: Project Site Plan

Site Name: Quintessential Poetry or Panacea Poetry

Site Purpose

The purpose of (Word Pending) Poetry is to provide users a place to learn and experience poetry. Taking it from the books and modernizing it with the internet. It will provide information about what poetry is and how it has been used historically. It will also provide how it is used in the modern day with techniques we imploy to create it. It will also provide the users with some modern and ancient examples of peotry. Finally, there will be a form for users to sign up for a mailing list that will send them a daily poem to encourage their day.

Scenarios

  • What are some of the techniques used to write poetry?
  • What is the definition of poetry?
  • How can I get started writing it?
